Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We‚Äôve got answers. Here are some of the most common things people ask.

What are the setup costs for hostel owners?

There are absolutely zero setup costs. Laundry Konnect covers all expenses for equipment, installation, training, and maintenance. It's a completely risk-free partnership for you.

How does the revenue sharing model work?

We operate on a simple revenue-sharing basis. You provide the space, and we provide the entire laundry solution. A portion of the revenue generated from each wash is shared with you, creating a new passive income stream for your hostel.

What happens if a machine breaks down?

Our dedicated support team is on standby. We provide full maintenance and will promptly service or repair any machine with minimal downtime, ensuring a consistently great experience for your students.

How much space is required for the setup?

The space required depends on the number of machines, but a typical setup can fit in a small room or even a corner of a common area (e.g., 10x10 feet). Our team will assess your site for free to determine the best location.

How do students pay for the laundry service?

Students pay conveniently and securely through any UPI-enabled app on their smartphone. The process is completely cashless, quick, and integrated into our system for easy tracking.
